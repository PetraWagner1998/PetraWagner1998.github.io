Einhell

An exciting project I have been heavily involved in is the website for Einhell. The site includes over 42 language tenants, spread across seven different instances and data centers. In this project, I had the opportunity to implement the majority of the frontend myself. I was also able to support many areas in the backend, where I developed various extensions, including product management, the blog, and the press portal.

The project also involves single sign-on, PIM, DAM, translation services, Solr, Zoovu, and various RESTful APIs for after-sales services especially sylius, dealer integrations, and SAP services.

Since 2022, I have been the Lead Developer for all our Einhell projects, working closely with the internal technical and marketing teams to ensure that all requirements are implemented smoothly.

Rent and travel

Rent and Travel is a booking platform for renting caravans and motorhomes, built on the powerful TYPO3 CMS. The entire booking process, designed to provide users with a seamless and intuitive experience, is implemented entirely in Vue.js. A key feature of the website is its smooth integration with the internal dealer booking system, ensuring an efficient rental process.

Last year, my colleagues and I had the exciting opportunity to give the brand a fresh, modern appearance. We succeeded in making the website shine in a new light, significantly improving both its user-friendliness and visual appeal. It was a fantastic project where I was able to contribute my technical expertise in both frontend and backend, while collaborating with a wonderful team.

TFK Buggy

TFK was my first Shopware 6 project and thus a true milestone for me. It gave me the unique opportunity to dive into a completely new technology and expand my knowledge in it. I was able to work through the entire shop process from the very beginning, designing the homepage to optimizing the checkout process. Along the way, I created new custom entities, extended existing ones, and fully immersed myself in the exciting Shopware Experience Worlds.

One particularly challenging and rewarding aspect of the project was managing multiple sales channels, each with different pricing structures, including net prices and multiple currencies. This complexity required me to thoroughly understand the pricing logic and the specific needs of each sales channel to ensure a seamless and consistent experience across the board.

It was fascinating to explore the various product features and display options, aligning them with the customer’s specific requirements. Through this project, I gained invaluable experience and significantly expanded my technical expertise in Shopware 6. It sparked my enthusiasm for e-commerce and Shopware

Eterna

The Eterna project was probably the one I enjoyed the most. The focus was on conveying emotions and creating a website that not only functions well but also provides users with an aesthetically pleasing and emotionally engaging experience. The designer and I had complete creative freedom when it came to deciding on the animations and designing the elements.

It was a special project where we could fully realize our ideas without limitations, resulting in a website that bursts with life and dynamism, perfectly conveying Eterna’s brand message in a unique way.

ODU

ODU is a leading provider of high-performance connector systems, with a website that features a complex product database containing millions of products. At the heart of the site is the Product Finder, a large-scale Vue.js application that allows users to search for the perfect connectors and solutions. I was primarily responsible for the development of this application.

One of the biggest challenges in this project was organizing and presenting the vast amount of data-millions of products-efficiently and clearly. It was crucial to structure the information in a user-friendly way while ensuring that it was delivered within an acceptable loading time, despite the sheer volume.

Knaus Microsites

One of my most challenging projects was undoubtedly the new landing pages for Knaus and Weinsberg. This exciting endeavor presented me with the demanding task of creating a modern and unique user experience that perfectly reflects the brand identities of both companies. It was essential to design an interface that not only captivates visually but is also intuitive to navigate, engaging users from the moment they arrive.

One of the biggest challenges was balancing these impressive design concepts with acceptable loading times. I had to ensure that the pages were not only aesthetically pleasing but also technically optimized to deliver quick load times. This is where the extensive use of GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) came into play, allowing me to implement animations that greatly enhanced the user experience. This powerful animation library enabled me to create smooth and engaging transitions that elevated the overall experience without compromising the site’s performance.
